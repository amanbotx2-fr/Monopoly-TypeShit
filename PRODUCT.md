# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Friends, families, and strategy-game players who want to create or join a real-time browser game without account setup. Hosts need to configure rooms and boards quickly; players need clear multiplayer presence, reliable reconnects, and low-friction access from desktop or mobile.

## Product Purpose

MONOPOLY is an independent multiplayer strategy platform for creating rooms, playing familiar property-trading games, spectating, and building custom boards. Success means the browser experience feels as polished and trustworthy as a dedicated game client while keeping room creation, joining, and play effortless.

## Brand Personality

Calm, architectural, and competitive. The product should communicate premium craftsmanship, strategic focus, social presence, and quiet confidence.

## Anti-references

Avoid childish board-game styling, casino cues, skeuomorphic pieces, mascot-led branding, neon-heavy gaming interfaces, dense dashboards, decorative glassmorphism, loud gradients, and generic browser-game presentation. The visual system should not compete with gameplay or obscure the primary action.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next meaningful action obvious.
2. Preserve familiar workflows while raising their perceived quality.
3. Treat multiplayer presence and connection state as first-class information.
4. Use restrained, purposeful feedback so every interaction feels responsive.
5. Keep the board and strategic decisions visually dominant during play.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, screen-reader labels for icon controls, large touch targets, high text contrast, and reduced-motion preferences. Do not communicate state through color alone, and keep mobile layouts gesture-friendly without overlapping controls.
